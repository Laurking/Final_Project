About

MariAlex is a free coding school offering classes to people with no or little programming skills. The classes will be taught by dedicated volunteers or people willing to share their programming knowledge.

Objectives

The goal of MariAlex coding school is to improve the programming world and facilitate in-person teaching to people with no means to attend a school. It will also help programmers network by locations, i.e cities and states.
It's online resource will help share tons of great tools to learn and teach. Those resources will include and will not be limited to ebooks, and videos.

Why MariAlex ?

MariAlex will provide jobs search and internship programs to students and learners endowed with skills required by the programming field. It wil also help connect students (with strong seriousness of purpose) with mentors and schools offering scholarships for them to continue their education.

Location

MariAlex, originally opened for the City of New York, will be extended to places having little or more interests in learning how to program. In addition, MariAlex will lay emphasis on children and establish a partnership with Public Libraries where the teachings and tutoring services will take place.

Programming languages

The programming languages that will be taught are: Python, Ruby, Javascript, java C++, Jquery, Swift, Objective C. Note: students will also be taught how to use HTM and CSS.
